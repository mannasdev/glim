## Roles (Owner, Admin, Member)

recce workspaces have three roles.

- **Owner** has full control of the workspace, including billing, the plan, and deleting the organization. There is always at least one Owner.
- **Admin** can manage tests, schedules, API keys, and members (invite, change roles, remove people) but doesn't own billing or org deletion.
- **Member** can create and run tests, edit schedules, and view results. Members can't manage other people or workspace-level settings.

Pick the least-privileged role that lets someone do their job. You can change a person's role later from the Members page.

## Inviting teammates

Open the Members page and click Invite. Enter your teammate's email, choose a role, and send the invite. They get a link to join the Acme workspace — once they accept, they show up in the members list with the role you picked.

Invite people with their work email so the member list stays readable, and prefer Member for most teammates. Reserve Admin for the folks who actually manage tests and keys day to day.
