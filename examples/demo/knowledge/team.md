# Team and roles

## Inviting a teammate

Go to the Team page and press Invite. Enter your teammate's email in the
dialog and send it — they receive a link that joins them to your workspace.
Invites expire after 7 days and can be re-sent from the same dialog.

## Roles

Harbor has three roles. Owners control billing, publishing, and team
membership. Managers can edit listings, respond to guests, and adjust
pricing, but cannot change billing. Cleaners see the cleaning calendar and
turnover checklists only.

## Removing someone

Only an Owner can remove a teammate. Removal takes effect immediately and
does not delete any work they did on your listings.
