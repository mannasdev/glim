# Billing and payouts

## Plans

Harbor Starter is free for one live listing. Harbor Pro is $29 per month
and covers up to twenty live listings, team roles, and priority support.
You can switch plans at any time; changes prorate automatically.

## Payouts

Payouts are sent to your linked bank account 24 hours after each guest
checks in. Enable the payout confirmation email in Settings if you want a
note every time money lands.

## Invoices

Monthly invoices are emailed to the workspace Owner and are also available
on request from support. Harbor charges no per-booking commission on Pro.
