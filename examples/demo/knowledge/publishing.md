# Publishing a listing

## What publishing does

A draft listing is only visible to you and your team. Publishing makes it
visible to guests on Harbor search, opens its calendar for bookings, and
starts nightly rate syncing. You can unpublish at any time without losing
photos, pricing, or reviews.

## How to publish

Open the Listings page, find the listing marked with a Draft badge, and
press its Publish button. The badge flips to Live immediately. Harbor runs
a quick quality check first: a listing needs a title, a nightly rate, and
at least one photo before the Publish button does anything.

## Unpublishing

Open the listing, choose Manage, then Unpublish. Existing confirmed
bookings are honored; the listing simply stops taking new ones.
